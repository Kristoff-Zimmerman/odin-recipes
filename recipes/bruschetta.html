Bruschetta

Bruschetta is an antipasto (starter dish) from Italy consisting of grilled bread rubbed with garlic and topped with olive oil and salt. Variations may include toppings of tomato, vegetables, beans, cured meat, or cheese. In Italy, bruschetta is often prepared using a brustolina grill.

Ingredients

  • 4 roma tomatoes, diced
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 small red onion, chopped
  • 1/2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 4 basil leaves, chopped
  • french bread
  • goat cheese, crumbled
  • balsamic glaze

Instructions

  1. Chop all and prepare tomatoes, garlic, basil, and onion
  2. Add to medium sized mixing bowl with olive oil and balsamic vinegar and mix
  3. Slice french bread and toast with a drizzle of olive oil
  4. spread mixture on bread slices and drizzle with balsamic glaze
